About Valery

Hi, I’m Gomolemo (Valery). I bake with love from my home kitchen in Mokwena Maswikeng, a small village outside Bochum near Kroemhoek. I started Valery’s Pastries to bring joy to local celebrations — from simple cookies to full custom cakes. Every order is made fresh to your request.

Serving Bochum (Senwabarwana) and nearby villages. For special requests, le ka kgokagana le rena ka WhatsApp — (we'll make it happen).
